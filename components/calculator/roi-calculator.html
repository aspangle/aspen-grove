Calculation Methodology

Our ROI calculator uses a sophisticated model based on data from hundreds of organizations using our platform:

  • Efficiency Gain: We apply a variable efficiency rate based on your document volume. Organizations typically see 40-60% time savings with our AI tools.
  • Additional Capacity: Calculated as your current volume multiplied by our efficiency factor, which accounts for diminishing returns at higher volumes.
  • Revenue Impact: The product of your additional capacity, hours per document, and hourly rate.
  • ROI Percentage: Annual revenue increase or cost savings divided by the annual subscription cost of our platform.

Results may vary based on industry, document complexity, and implementation factors.
