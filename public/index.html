Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Aspen Grove's secure professional service management platform.

What services can I request through the platform?

Our platform connects you with trusted service providers for various professional services including document processing, analysis, consulting, and specialized business services. Services vary by provider but commonly include document review, research, reporting, and consultation services across legal, healthcare, insurance, and business sectors.

How secure is my data on the platform?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-grade encryption (AES-256) for all data, maintain HIPAA compliance, and are SOC 2 certified. All communications are encrypted end-to-end, access is controlled with role-based permissions, and detailed audit logs track all platform activity. Your data is hosted on secure AWS infrastructure with 99.9% uptime.

Can professionals from any industry use this platform?

Yes! Aspen Grove is designed to work across industries. We serve professionals in legal, healthcare, insurance, real estate, finance, and many other sectors. The platform adapts to your industry's specific needs while maintaining universal features like secure document management, communication tools, and service request tracking.

How does pricing work?

We offer flexible subscription plans based on your usage needs and team size. Plans include features like secure document storage, service request management, and communication tools. Pricing varies between clients (who request services) and service providers (who fulfill requests). Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

What compliance standards does the platform meet?

Aspen Grove meets multiple industry compliance standards including HIPAA for healthcare data, SOC 2 for security controls, and follows best practices for financial services and legal industries. We conduct regular security audits, maintain detailed audit logs, and provide compliance reporting to help you meet your regulatory requirements.
